Mission Statement: My goal is to never stop learning and growing. I want to be able to provide for a family and be an active member of society, promoting all things that are good. I especially want to excel in writing and producing music. My goal is to make music- not just to play it. Expressing feelings and life and color throughout the world is my end goal.
